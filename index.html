Interesting Facts About the Camel

  • They store fat in their humps.
  • They can drink lots of water in one go!
  • Their feet are wide so that they can easily walk on sand - just like snowshoes!

Interesting Facts About the Chameleon

  • They can change their colour depending on their mood!
  • Their tongues move very fast and are very sticky, so they can catch insects with them!
  • They are very good climbers, and have eyes that can move independently!
